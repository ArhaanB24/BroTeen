Bulking Diet

  • • Scrambled eggs (3-4 eggs) with vegetables (1 cup)
    • 2 whole wheat toast slices
    • 1 glass of milk (250ml)

  • • Serving of Greek yogurt (200-250g) with honey or fruit.

  • • Grilled chicken breast or fish (200-250g) with a side of mixed vegetables (1-1.5 cups)
    • Brown rice or quinoa (1 cup)
    • A salad with cucumbers, carrots, and tomatoes

  • • Protein shake with whey protein (1-2 scoops) mixed with water or milk.

  • • A banana or other piece of fruit
    • A serving of mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts, or cashews) or a small portion of cottage cheese

  • • Boiled chicken (200-250g) with grilled vegetables (1-1.5 cups)
    • Whole wheat roti (2) or brown rice (1 cup). • Raita with yogurt, cucumber, and mint (1/2 cup).

  • • Stay hydrated with water or herbal teas throughout the day.
    • Include healthy fats from sources like avocados, olive oil, and nuts.
    • Monitor your progress and make necessary adjustments in portion sizes and food choices to meet your bulking goals.
    • Engage in regular strength training to complement your diet and promote muscle growth.
